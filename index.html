Çağıl Koçyiğit

Email: cagil.kocyigit@epfl.ch

I am currently a PhD student in the team of Risk Analytics and Optimization at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). My research focuses on mechanism design and robust optimization. Previously, I received a B.Sc. and an M.Sc. in Industrial Engineering from Bilkent University in 2013 and 2015, respectively.
