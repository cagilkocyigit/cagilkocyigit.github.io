I am an Assistant Professor at the Luxembourg Centre for Logistics and Supply Chain Management, University of Luxembourg. My research focuses on optimization under uncertainty with applications in economics and mechanism design. Prior to joining University
of Luxembourg, I received a Ph.D. in Management of Technology from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in 2020,
where I worked with Daniel Kuhn. I hold a B.Sc. degree and an M.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineering from Bilkent University.

Key areas of interest. optimization under uncertainty: (distributionally) robust optimization, stochastic and dynamic programming,
online learning; game theory and mechanism design: auction design, pricing, contract design

Prospective students. I am looking for motivated PhD students with strong mathematical background. Interested candidates may
contact me via email.
